Site Name: Family Heritage Discovery

This site focuses on helping people discover their family heritage. After the completion of the assignment, the site will be hosted at familyheritagediscovery.org.

Purpose

familyheritagediscovery.org will be a persistent resource for people who want to learn about family history generally, and about their families specificlaly. As it grows, it will include a moderated community space where people can ask for research help. It will also include basic productivity tools focused on doing family history research.

Scenarios

  • I am new to family history. What is the best way to start learning about my family?
  • How can I start building my family tree?
  • What kind of free resources exist that will help me find my ancestors?
