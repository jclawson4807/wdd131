Online Sources

Family SearchJames Clawson

Family Heritage Discovery focuses on helping people discover their family heritage. Once development is complete, the site will be hosted at familyheritagediscovery.org. Our site is for people who want to learn about family history generally, and about their families specifically. You can sign up for our weekly newsletter, which will be written by experienecd researchers and software developers who help build family history tools. Our newsletter will provide weekly tips and family history relatead news. We will provide articles about gathering family history information from family members, step by step how-tos to help you with research, and more. We are working on a video series in which our researchers will help new genealogists sign up on research sites, do family interviews, enter information into online family trees, and solve difficult research dilemas. Who knows, maybe we can help you! We envision a day when the site will include a moderated community space where people can ask for research help, as well as basic productivity tools focused on doing family history research.
