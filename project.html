Site Name: Family Heritage Discovery

This site focuses on helping people discover their family heritage. After the completion of the assignment, the site will be hosted at familyheritagediscovery.org. The logo shown below will be adapted to become a favicon as well.

Purpose

familyheritagediscovery.org will be a persistent resource for people who want to learn about family history generally, and about their families specifically. The page will include a form which will allow visitors to join our mailing list. Eventually, after the assignment is done, the site will include a moderated community space where people can ask for research help, as well as basic productivity tools focused on doing family history research.

Scenarios

  • I am new to family history. What is the best way to start learning about my family?
  • How can I start building my family tree?
  • What kind of free resources exist that will help me find my ancestors?

Page Design

The three content screens will all display content using the same section template. The body of an article may contain an image. For the purposes of this assignment, the article data will be included in an array of Javascript objects. The selection of different screens from the menu will result in the array being filtered by a screen type value. In later iterations of this page, the content will be stored in a database and loaded based on user search queries or by page association.
